Customer Reviews

Marcus B. @marcusb

Great variety of cold desserts at Ohh Wee Ice Cream. I tried the chocolate fudge sundae, and it was deliciously decadent. The place is clean, and the prices are reasonable. My only suggestion would be to offer more non-dairy options for those with dietary restrictions. Other than that, it's a wonderful spot for a treat.

Samantha L. @samanthal

I am absolutely in love with Ohh Wee Ice Cream! Their vanilla bean ice cream is the creamiest I've ever had, and the toppings bar is fantastic. The staff goes out of their way to make sure you have a great experience. This place is a gem, and I can't recommend it enough!

Jason T. @jasont

The yogurt here is top-notch, especially the Greek yogurt with honey and walnuts. The portion sizes are generous, and the quality is consistent. My only gripe is that the lines can get quite long during peak hours. But if you don't mind a bit of a wait, it's definitely worth it.

Emily R. @emilyr

Ohh Wee Ice Cream has a delightful selection of flavors! I particularly enjoyed the mango sorbet, which was refreshing and not too sweet. The staff was friendly and the ambiance was pleasant. However, I wish they had more seating options for larger groups. Overall, a great place to satisfy your sweet tooth!
